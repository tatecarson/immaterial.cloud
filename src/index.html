This is music you must hear with friends. Point three other phones to this page and enter in the ID of the first phone. Now your phones are all connected and ready to play together.

Put each phone in a different part of the room to specialize the sound. Hold your hand over each phone to see how it effects the music. If you want you can experiment with putting the phones in boxes to see how they sound. When you raise the lid of the box you will trigger a change in the music.
